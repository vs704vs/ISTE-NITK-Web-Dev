BeatStars is a global music marketplace offering everything needed to help music creators and entrepreneurs get started growing their business. From beats to vocals, sound kits to services, BeatStars lets you license and sell your musical content, empowering creators from around the world to make their mark.

Members can monetize music by selling beats, songs, and products through our premier all-in-one online marketplace and customizable Pro Page storefront, which includes advanced search engines, built-in media players, and more. Access cutting edge tools and integrations, as well as in-depth analytics to scale your business quickly and keep your customers coming back for more. Learn about music and entrepreneurship through interactive live shows and other educational content, regularly provided for free.

BeatStars is a vital hub for music producers, recording artists, engineers, and creators of all levels to build their brand and connect with a community of global creators.
